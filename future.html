  • Real-time GPS tracking
  • AI-powered health monitoring
  • Sleep and activity analysis
  • Smart alerts for unusual behavior
  • Integrated camera support
  • AI assistant for pet owners
  • Community platform for dog owners
  • Future veterinary AI integration

Technology Stack: Python, Firebase, IoT systems, embedded hardware integration, AI technologies, database systems, and frontend development.
